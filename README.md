Computer Engineering Student | Hardware Engineer | FPGA, RTL, Embedded Systems
------------------------------------------------------------------------------

I'm a Computer Engineering student at the University of Central Florida focused on digital hardware, FPGA systems, RTL design, design verification, and embedded systems. My projects span RISC-V execution-stage RTL, custom CPU design, AXI/DMA-based FPGA accelerators, DSP filters, embedded sensor processing, and AI-assisted RTL research.

I enjoy building hardware from multiple angles: writing Verilog/SystemVerilog RTL, verifying designs with self-checking testbenches and assertions, scripting simulations with Python/Bash, modeling DSP behavior in MATLAB, and bringing systems closer to real FPGA or embedded hardware. I'm especially interested in roles involving FPGA development, digital design, hardware verification, embedded systems, and computer architecture.

* 🌍  I'm based in United States
* 🧠  I'm currently learning Advanced SystemVerilog verification, FPGA DSP design, AXI/DMA systems, PetaLinux, and hardware acceleration workflows
* 👥  I'm looking to collaborate on FPGA projects, RTL design, embedded systems, digital hardware, computer architecture, and hardware verification projects
* 💬  Ask me about I like working close to the hardware: datapaths, FSMs, waveforms, timing, buses, peripherals, and the annoying bugs that only show up when simulation, tooling, or real hardware gets involved.
